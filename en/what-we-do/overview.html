What We Do

The Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum Global Initiatives’ entities and initiatives are classified under five main pillars, each serving a particular humanitarian, social and development sector to meet the Foundation’s overarching mission to build stable and prosperous societies. The pillars reflect MBRGI’s vision to create hope and foster a culture of positive change in the region.

The five main pillars are:

  • Humanitarian Aid and Relief
  • Healthcare and Disease Control
  • Spreading Education and Knowledge
  • Innovation and Entrepreneurship
  • Empowering Communities

Humanitarian Aid and Relief

This pillar reflects the core mission of being an overarching entity of humanitarian entities and initiatives that seek to improve lives and deploy necessary resources and capacities to alleviate all kinds of suffering across the world.

Healthcare and Disease Control

This pillar reflects the MBRGI’s immense regional and global efforts to improve quality of life, tackle the most pressing medical challenges that hinder development and create environments that nurture stable and prosperous societies.

Spreading Education and Knowledge

The initiatives and projects within this pillar are dedicated to developing educational systems, especially in the Arab world, and facilitate necessary resources to create innovative and high quality school environments.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Through this pillar, MBRGI supports initiatives, programmes and projects that promote entrepreneurial thinking and creativity, in efforts to create an impactful economic and social shift that contributes to the development of local communities and, on a wider scope, raise aspirations of global societies.

Empowering Communities

The core mission of this pillar is to empower communities with the necessary tools for their development and prosperity. The MBRGI guides communities through the challenges that impede progress and development, making a positive impact on people’s lives and reinforcing a culture of hope.
